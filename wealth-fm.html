About Wealth FM

Wealth FM (WFM) is a Pan-African bilingual (Swahili and English) financial empowerment radio station under Wealth Media Group (WMG). Based in Dar es Salaam, Tanzania, WFM broadcasts 24/7 across FM and digital platforms with transformative content designed to advance financial literacy, economic participation, digital inclusion, and wealth creation.

WFM empowers listeners to take charge of their financial future by offering practical knowledge, inspirational stories, and real opportunities.

Program Categories & Flagship Shows

Financial Literacy & Inclusion

  • The Money Circle / Mduara wa Pesa

    Focuses on VICOBA, SACCOs, mobile money, and the power of saving culture.

  • Citizen Capital / Mtaji wa Mwananchi

    Real stories of financial inclusion, local investments, and IPO access.

  • Banking Made Easy / Benki Rahisi

    Demystifying banks, digital wallets, loans, savings, and credit management.

Youth, Career & Entrepreneurship Development

  • Rise & Rewire / Amka Kijana

    Personal growth, mindset training, and morning purpose alignment.

  • Gen Z Nation / Kizazi Kipya

    Youth innovation, financial independence, and leadership journeys.

  • Ajira Express / Kazi Leo

    Practical job search tips, CV and interview coaching, and HR insights.

  • Digital Mama / Mama Dijitali

    Empowering women with tools for digital entrepreneurship and financial resilience.

Creative Economy & Digital Monetization

  • Play to Pay / Cheza na Lipwa

    Showcasing income generation through music, content creation, sports, and gaming.

  • Nightlight Africa / Usiku na Mwanga Afrika

    Creative soul hour featuring spoken word, jazz, poetry, and musical expression.

  • The Gold Mindset / Akili ya Dhahabu

    Exploring habits, beliefs, and routines of successful African entrepreneurs.

Investment & Market Intelligence

  • Wealth & Markets / Masoko na Utajiri

    Business trends, stock markets, CEO conversations, and investment insights.

  • Fursa 360 / Fursa360

    Tenders, grants, scholarships, trade alerts, and breakthrough opportunities.

  • The Legacy Line / Urithi wa Kesho

    Multi-generational wealth, family enterprises, and financial heritage.

Faith & Purpose-Driven Finance

  • Purpose & Provision / Imani na Uchumi

    Integrating faith, integrity, and prosperity in personal and business finances.
