Welcome to WTV

Africa's Business Intelligence Powerhouse

WTV (Wealth Television) is a bilingual Pan-African economic broadcasting network under Wealth Media Group (WMG), headquartered in Dar es Salaam, Tanzania. Anchored in the belief that wealth creation begins with knowledge, WTV is committed to empowering individuals, SMEs, corporations, policymakers, and investors across Africa and its diaspora through premium economic content.

We bring you high-impact programming in Swahili (70%) and English (30%)—fusing data-driven journalism, human-centered storytelling, innovation showcases, investment opportunities, and live interactive shows that move markets, minds, and movements.

Whether you're a student, side hustler, CEO, policy analyst, or diaspora investor—WTV is where Africa's economy becomes your economy.
