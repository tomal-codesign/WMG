Wealth TV – Made in Africa

WTV is the flagship television network of Wealth Media Group — a bold and bilingual business intelligence channel offering 24/7 programming in Swahili and English

Through documentaries, explainer videos, live studio conversations, and data-driven storytelling, Wealth TV covers the entire spectrum of Africa’s growth journey — from policy and markets to innovation and public-private partnerships

WTV’s Content Categories

  • Economic policy & capital markets
  • Entrepreneurship & MSME growth
  • Innovation, digital economy, and creative industries
  • Diaspora engagement & global development
  • Financial literacy & national transformation

Wealth FM – Ni Yako

WFM is the 24/7 financial empowerment radio platform of Wealth Media Group — designed for the masses and the markets. Broadcasting in Swahili and English, it connects listeners with opportunity, insight, and inspiration wherever they are.

WFM Programming

  • Financial literacy & investment know-how
  • Career development & youth empowerment
  • Creative economy & digital monetization
  • Diaspora finance & cross-border trade
  • Women, family, and grassroots wealth

With interactive elements like SMS polls, WhatsApp diaries, Twitter Spaces, and TikTok contests, WFM makes wealth talk real, accessible, and local.
