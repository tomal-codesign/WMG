Executive Summary

Wealth Magazine, published by Wealth Media Group (WMG), is Africa’s definitive financial publication. With dual-language editions (English and Kiswahili), it delivers monthly print and digital content dedicated to wealth creation, investment intelligence, economic empowerment, and strategic business reporting. Through feature-rich content, sector analyses, thought leadership, and exclusive interviews, it empowers investors, entrepreneurs, policymakers, the diaspora, women, youth, MSMEs, and development partners across Africa to make informed, bold, and future-facing financial decisions.

Content to Market (CTM) Strategy

Wealth Magazine is more than a publication—it is a deal flow accelerator and influence amplifier

  • Investor Exposure: Decision-makers, funders, and venture capital firms monitor our featured profiles
  • Product Placement: Strategic ad space and B2B/B2C showcases in “The Marketplace”
  • SME & Diaspora Connection: Stories that link African diaspora with emerging ventures and local investment clubs
  • Cross-Platform Integration: Featured businesses get media exposure via WFM (Wealth Radio), WTV, and our YouTube, LinkedIn, and podcast ecosystems
  • Executive Roundtables: Partners are invited to private sessions featured in “The Wealth Room”
  • Data Sponsorships: Financial institutions sponsor analytics in our “Investment Tracker” or “Sector Forecast” sections
  • Story-to-Capital Pipeline: Editors connect compelling business stories to capital networks
